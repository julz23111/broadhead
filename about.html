Features & Benefits

Everything you need in the field—engineered for speed, safety, and reliability.

Built for Archers, by Archers

Designed by an archer who knows the frustration of a missed shot and a stuck broadhead. Whether you're bowhunting or target shooting, the Broadhead Buddy™ is purpose-built to remove embedded broadheads safely and efficiently. No more using your arrow for leverage, prying with pliers, or chiseling around blades to free the head. These risky methods can break arrow shafts, bend ferrules, or chip blades. Broadhead Buddy™ eliminates the damage and gets your broadhead back.

The Broadhead Buddy™ is built to last and engineered for serious performance. Order yours below or ask your local pro shop to carry it.

Engineered Features & Benefits

Precision CNC Machining

Each tool is meticulously machined from a single billet of 304 stainless steel and 6061 aluminums, eliminating weak points and ensuring perfect function every time.

Mechanical Advantage

Uses controlled positive axial force multiplication. By applying a relatively small force to the knob the Broadhead Buddy generates a very large linear pulling force, allowing you to easily extract deeply embedded broadheads from even the densest objects.

Multi-Functionality

This isn't a one-trick pony. Broadhead Buddy is compatible with most fixed and mechanical 2,3 and 4 blade Broadheads; works on field points too. It can also save your expensive arrow shaft inserts. Don’t grab and damage the insert with pilers, this prevents them from fitting in the arrow correctly and true again. Instead use the included piece of all-thread rod. Thread it into the back of the insert and pull it like you would a broadhead.

Durability and Performance

Every detail is engineered for uncompromising performance and maximum longevity. Premium constructed stainless steel and aluminum make it corrosion resistant and far superior molded alternatives.
